## How to Trade Earnings — The Playbook

### Step 1: Signal check (day before)
```
P/C < 0.5 + ATM calls being bought + earnings tonight
= Buy 1-2 ATM calls with 5% of account
```

### Step 2: Choose the right option
```
ATM call (delta ~0.5) = best for earnings
  Not too expensive, not a lottery ticket
  Moves 50 cents per $1 stock move
```

### Step 3: Size correctly
```
Max risk = 5% of account
$15K account → max $750 per earnings trade
= 1-2 contracts only
Never bet more — IV crush can kill even correct direction
```

### Step 4: Exit immediately
```
After earnings announcement → sell pre-market
Don't hold through IV crush
IV drops from 100% → 30% after earnings
Even correct direction can lose if IV crushes
```

### Step 5: The signals that worked
```
GOOGL P/C 0.29 → +7.3% ✅ (signal was correct)
AMZN P/C 0.44 → +3.0% ✅ (signal was correct)
META P/C 0.88 → -7.3% ✅ (straddle = uncertain, correct)
MSFT P/C 0.46 → -3.1% ❌ (bullish signal, missed)
```

**3 out of 4 signals were correct direction.**

## Lessons

1. **When P/C < 0.5 before earnings → buy ATM call** (small size)
2. **Straddle pattern (P/C ~1.0) → skip or buy straddle**
3. **Exit pre-market, don't hold through IV crush**
4. **GOOGL was in our scanner — signal was there, just not acted on**
5. **Add earnings trades to flow_trader or manual watchlist**
